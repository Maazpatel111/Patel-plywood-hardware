About Us

Everything Your Space Needs,
Under One Roof

Patel Plywood & Hardware, Solapur, is your trusted destination for interior and construction materials. Whether you're furnishing a single room on a tight budget or crafting a high-end luxury interior — we stock quality options across every price point, for homeowners, contractors, and designers alike.

Our showroom carries 500+ products across six categories — ISI-certified Plywood & Boards, designer Laminates, natural Veneers, decorative Louvers, Modular Kitchen Panels, and premium Hardware fittings. We stock both aspirational top brands and smart-value alternatives, so you always find what fits your project and your budget.

  • Free expert guidance on every product selection
  • Economy to luxury — options for every budget
  • Bulk & contractor trade pricing available
  • ISI-certified & top-brand products guaranteed
