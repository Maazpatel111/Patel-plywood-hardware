About Us

Building Trust Since 1999

Patel Plywood & Hardware is a family-owned showroom dedicated to providing the finest quality plywood, laminates, veneers, and hardware accessories. We believe every project deserves the best material.

From interior designers to contractors and home builders, we serve all with competitive pricing and expert guidance.

  • ISI-marked & branded products only
  • Bulk and retail both available
  • Free home delivery on orders above ₹5,000
  • Expert advice & after-sales support
